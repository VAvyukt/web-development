Youtube

Youtube was established on Febuary 14th, 2005, in San Mateo, CA. It was established by Jawed Karim, Chad Hurley, and Steve Chen. Today, Youtube is owned by Google. This site allows content creators to post videos on the web for the world to view. Youtube also allows fans to subscribe to their creators and watch their videos as soon as they are released. Youtube has become so well known that content creators that make content for Youtube are called YouTubers.
