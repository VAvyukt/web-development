About the Coding Club

We are a community of students passionate about technology, programming, and innovation. Whether you're a seasoned coder or just starting your journey, our club provides a supportive environment to learn, collaborate on exciting projects, and prepare for a future in tech. Join us for engaging workshops, hackathons, and networking opportunities designed to build your skills and connect you with like-minded peers. Explore our site to find meeting schedules, project showcases, and resources to help you dive into the world of coding!
