A Trip Around the Globe

Africa

Republic of the Congo

  • Official Language: French
  • Currency: Central African CFA franc (XAF)
  • Fun Fact: Established Independence from France in 1960

Republic of South Africa (South Africa)

  • Official Languages: Afrikaans, Zulu, Xhosa, Pedi, Southern Sotho, Tswana, Tsonga, Swati, Venda, Southern Ndebele
  • Currency: South African rand (ZAR)
  • Fun Fact: Southernmost country in Africa

North America

United States of America (USA)

  • Official Language: English
  • Currency: United States dollar (USD)
  • Fun Fact: It has the world's third-largest land area

United Mexican States (Mexico)

  • Official Language: Spanish
  • Currency: Mexican peso (MXN)
  • Fun Fact: Northernmost country in Latin America
